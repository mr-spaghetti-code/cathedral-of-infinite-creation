About

Enter the "Cathedral of Infinite Creation," a tool designed to offer a 3D-visualized journey through the constantly evolving world of AI-generated content. It was built to evoke the never-ending stream of content - a small glimpse into the human psyche. By ingesting tens of thousands of creations from MidJourney we can create an experience that I feel conveys the richness of human-machine creativity.

Images are associated by computing embeddings on their prompt and calculating a semantic similarity score.

Every few seconds, another image is "dropped" for you to see.

This is a desktop-optimized experience but it also works on high-end phones.
